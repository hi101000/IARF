What is ASBOT?

ASBOT (Aevum Secundī Bellī Orbis Terrārum) is a history of the Second World War written entirely in Latin (except citations, which are in English)
I aim for it to be one of the most complete histories of the war, drawing on a wide range of both primary and secondary sources.
